# Marketing Strategy Consulting Case Study: Equoia Renewable Energy Expansion

## Business Problem
Equoia needed a structured market entry and expansion plan for renewable energy solutions across multiple sectors, with clear commercial logic and an India expansion pathway.

## Stakeholder Requirements
- Leadership: sector prioritisation and market entry rationale.
- Finance: cost-benefit model and investment sequencing.
- Operations: implementation roadmap and expansion dependencies.
- Sustainability stakeholders: alignment with regulatory, policy and sustainability goals.

## Analysis & Artefacts
- Market entry and expansion planning across target sectors.
- Cost-benefit and financial models to support strategic decision-making.
- India expansion roadmap aligned with policy, regulation and sustainability priorities.
- Executive narrative translating market complexity into clear choices.

## Executive Recommendation
Prioritise sectors with stronger policy tailwinds, clearer adoption economics and lower implementation friction. Sequence expansion through pilots before committing to broader market coverage.

## Measurable Outcomes
Decision-ready expansion roadmap, commercial model and strategic recommendations for renewable energy growth.
