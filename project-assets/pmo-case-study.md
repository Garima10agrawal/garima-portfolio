# PMO Case Study: Digital Delivery Portfolio Control Tower

## Business Problem
A digital delivery team needed consistent status reporting, time-tracking governance, lifecycle documentation and clearer escalation pathways.

## Stakeholder Requirements
- Senior PM: reliable weekly portfolio view.
- Delivery Team: clear SOPs and fewer rework loops.
- Finance: accurate time tracking inputs.
- Client Lead: concise status and risk visibility.

## Analysis & Artefacts
- Data sources: Asana tasks, Everhour time entries, project lifecycle files and meeting actions.
- User stories: weekly portfolio view, SOP clarity and simple RAG reporting.
- Process map: plan, assign owners, track time, update RAG, review risks and report.
- Dashboard: milestone adherence, open risks, timesheet completion and priority escalations.
- KPIs: milestone adherence, RAG movement, overdue actions, cycle time, risk ageing and documentation quality.

## Executive Recommendation
Standardise weekly status definitions, introduce a portfolio control tower, maintain SOPs in a single source of truth and run a risk review before sprint reviews.

## Measurable Outcomes
Improved project visibility, cleaner PMO lifecycle documentation, stronger time-tracking governance and faster escalation of blockers.
