# Business Analyst Case Study: Inclusive BNPL App Uplift

## Business Problem
A BNPL provider needed to improve onboarding, spending control and customer trust for underserved customers while maintaining responsible lending expectations.

## Stakeholder Requirements
- Product: inclusive onboarding and clearer value proposition.
- Risk: transparent affordability and repayment controls.
- Marketing: segment-specific acquisition messages.
- Customer Support: reduced repayment confusion.

## Analysis & Artefacts
- Data reviewed: market research across three regions, funnel assumptions, competitor benchmarks and trust-related survey themes.
- User stories: plain-language repayment details, spending limits, reminders and product funnel visibility.
- Future-state process: discover, eligibility check, budget setup, merchant browse, repayment reminders and support.
- Wireframe: mobile spend planner with available balance and reminder controls.
- KPIs: onboarding completion, activation, first purchase conversion, repayment reminder adoption, arrears rate and CSAT.

## Executive Recommendation
Ship plain-language onboarding, add proactive budget controls, test inclusive messaging by segment and maintain a repayment health dashboard.

## Measurable Outcomes
Projected 15% market share growth, lower onboarding drop-off, higher activation and stronger customer confidence.
