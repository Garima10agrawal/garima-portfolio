# Product Analytics Case Study: Contoso Hotel Sentiment & Revenue Uplift

## Business Problem
Contoso Hotel had strong occupancy but declining guest sentiment, inconsistent service recovery and weak visibility into the drivers of repeat booking behaviour.

## Stakeholder Requirements
- General Manager: weekly NPS and sentiment trend by property.
- Revenue Manager: linkage between sentiment, ADR and repeat bookings.
- Operations Lead: service issue heatmap by category, property and shift.
- Marketing Lead: segment-level customer themes for retention campaigns.

## Analysis & Artefacts
- Data sources: guest reviews, post-stay surveys, booking history, service tickets and loyalty data.
- User stories: property-level trend monitoring, sentiment-to-revenue analysis and operational owner assignment.
- Process map: collect feedback, clean text, classify sentiment, join booking data, publish dashboard, assign actions.
- Financial model: repeat booking recovery, complaint compensation reduction and direct booking conversion uplift.
- KPIs: NPS, sentiment score, repeat booking rate, complaint SLA, direct booking mix, ADR impact and service recovery cost.

## Executive Recommendation
Prioritise check-in workflow redesign, housekeeping quality checks and automated guest recovery triggers. Introduce a weekly executive scorecard with named action owners.

## Measurable Outcomes
Projected 12-15% revenue uplift, 20% complaint reduction, 9.4% repeat booking improvement and faster operational prioritisation.
