# Business Consulting Case Study: Senoptix Market Entry & Growth Strategy

## Business Problem
Senoptix needed a validated growth pathway, commercial model and phased implementation plan before engaging partners and investors.

## Stakeholder Requirements
- Founder: investor-ready narrative and growth thesis.
- Operations: implementation roadmap and channel feasibility.
- Sales: priority segments, pricing tiers and target account logic.
- Finance: revenue scenarios and unit economics.

## Analysis & Artefacts
- Discovery: 12 stakeholder interviews and market research.
- Process map: discovery, interviews, market sizing, segmentation, financial model, roadmap.
- User stories: corporate buyer access, consumer price transparency and founder roadmap visibility.
- Financial model: conservative, base and upside revenue scenarios from $5M to $15M annual potential, including $500-$1,000 device pricing and $18-$50 test kit tiers.
- KPIs: CAC, gross margin, repeat purchase, pilot conversion, payback period, channel profitability and activation.

## Executive Recommendation
Lead with B2B pilots, use corporate wellness as the trust-building channel, defer vending expansion until demand density is proven and track unit economics weekly.

## Measurable Outcomes
Investor-ready 1-3 year roadmap, prioritised segment list, channel economics and scenario revenue model.
